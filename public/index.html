Game of Gods

The 2 apostles work on strict rules. Ask them yes/no game questions and unmask how they work! State their identities/rules clearly to end the game.

Objective: Find out the identities of the apostles! You can guess at their identity to Apostle A.

Hint #1

They only respond to questions about the game itself! Ask them questions about their identities or how they interact with you!

Hint #2

Hint #3
